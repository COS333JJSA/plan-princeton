Concentration Comparision: Operations Research and Financial Engineering (ORF)

Here's advice from Peer Academic Advisors on Comparing ECO and ORF:

Economics (ECO)

  • Economic theory, applications to the “real world”
  • No engineering prereqs
  • Can be easily applied interdisciplinarily to analyze data (ex. Medicine, etc)
  • Independent work includes “mini thesis” for JP

Operations Research and Financial Engineering (ORF)

  • Data, mathematical modeling, quantitative computer science
  • Engineering prereqs (PHY, CHM, etc)
  • Applied math, both on paper and through code
