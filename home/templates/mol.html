Concentration Comparision: Ecology and Evolutionary Biology (EEB)

Here's advice from Peer Academic Advisors on Comparing MOL and EEB:

Molecular Biology (MOL)

  • How things in the natural world function
  • Focus on biological functions and and processes on a small, mechanistic scale
  • Lab thesis not necessary -- non lab options available
  • Lab work usually focuses very specifically on elucidating processes or specific protein function

Ecology and Evolutionary Biology (EEB)

  • How the natural world came to be
  • Focus on larger systems
  • Possible to do molecular biology related thesis within the department (i.e. immunology)
  • More opportunity for field work or study abroad
