Concentration Comparision: Psychology (PSY)

Here's advice from Peer Academic Advisors on Comparing NEU and PSY:

Neuroscience (NEU)

  • Biology of why brain works
  • More prerequisites
  • Lab thesis is more likely -- staying on campus before senior year
  • Math/bio/computational modeling

Psychology (PSY)

  • How people think
  • More flexible
  • Still possible to do neuroscience based thesis or independent work
