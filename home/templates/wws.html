WWS

Woodrow Wilson School

Prerequisites for Departmental Concentration

Four courses: Microeconomics (EC0 100, ECO 300, ECO 310 or WWS 300); one HIS course (cross-listed courses included, but HA courses must be designated HIS to count); one POL, SOC or PSY at any level (cross-listed courses included); statistics (WWS 200, POL 345, POL 346, ECO 302, ECO 312 for class of 2021; class of 2020 may still use ECO 202 and ORF 245, but these provide less preparation for data analysis, an essential element of the WWS major). D grades, P/D/F courses and AP courses will not count.
